Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Linear Calendar ("we", "our", "the app") is a client-side web application that helps you view your Google Calendar in a linear, year-at-a-glance layout. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Access

When you sign in with Google, the app requests access to:

  • Google Calendar data (read and write) — to display, create, edit, and delete your calendar events.
  • Basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) — to display your account info in the app header.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively within your browser to render the calendar interface. Specifically:

  • Calendar events are fetched from Google's servers and displayed directly in your browser.
  • Profile information is shown in the app header for your convenience.
  • Theme preference and current year are saved in your browser's local storage.

3. Data Storage and Transmission

  • No server-side storage: Linear Calendar is a purely client-side application. We do not operate any backend servers or databases.
  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal data or calendar information with any third party.
  • OAuth tokens: Your Google access token is held only in browser memory and is never persisted to disk or sent to any server other than Google's APIs.
  • Local storage: Only non-sensitive preferences (theme, current year) are stored in your browser's local storage.

4. Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with the following Google services:

  • Google Identity Services (authentication)
  • Google Calendar API (calendar data)
  • Google UserInfo API (profile information)

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your calendar data flows directly between your browser and Google's servers.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not store any of your data, there is nothing to retain. When you close the browser tab or sign out, your access token is discarded.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke Linear Calendar's access to your Google account at any time:

  • Click "Sign out" in the app, or
  • Visit Google Account Permissions and remove Linear Calendar.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact
